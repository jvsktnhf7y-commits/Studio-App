Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

📅

Live Lesson Schedule

See today's lessons pulled directly from Google Calendar. Your schedule is always up to date — no double entry.

✅

One-Tap Attendance

Mark a lesson confirmed, missed, or cancelled in one tap. Fees are deducted from the student's balance automatically.

✏️

Lesson Notes

Write what you covered and assign homework right after each lesson. Parents and students see it instantly in their own apps.

💳

Payment Tracking

Log cash, Venmo, Zelle, or card payments on the spot. Student balances update in real time across all apps.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Portal App

Parents get their own app to see upcoming lessons, lesson notes, assignments, and their prepaid balance.

🎸

Student App

Students log in with a simple access code to view their upcoming schedule and all lesson notes from their teacher.
