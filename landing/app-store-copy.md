## 1. Teacher App (Maestro — Studio Manager)

**App Name:** Maestro: Music Studio Manager

**Subtitle (30 chars max):** Lessons, notes & payments

**Description:**
Maestro is the studio management app built for independent music teachers. Everything you need to run your studio is in your pocket.

MANAGE YOUR SCHEDULE
See every lesson pulled live from Google Calendar. Your schedule is always current — no double entry, no manual updates.

RECORD ATTENDANCE IN ONE TAP
Mark lessons as confirmed, missed, or cancelled instantly. Student balances update automatically based on your rates.

WRITE LESSON NOTES ON THE SPOT
Capture what you worked on and assign homework right after each lesson. Parents and students see it in their own apps the moment you save.

LOG PAYMENTS ANYWHERE
Accept cash, Venmo, Zelle, or card? Log it from your phone and student balances update in real time.

STUDENT PROFILES AT A GLANCE
See any student's balance, attendance history, upcoming lessons, and notes with one tap. Generate their Student app access code directly from their profile.

THREE APPS, ONE STUDIO
Maestro includes a Parent App and Student App so families stay connected to lessons without you having to text or email updates.

BUILT FOR INDEPENDENT TEACHERS
No bloat. No complicated setup. Connect your Google Calendar, add your students, and you're running in minutes.

**Keywords:** music teacher, studio management, lesson tracker, attendance, music lessons, piano teacher, guitar teacher, lesson notes, student management, music studio

**Category:** Business

**Rating:** 4+

## 2. Parent App (Maestro — Parent Portal)

**App Name:** Maestro: Parent Portal

**Subtitle (30 chars max):** Stay connected to lessons

**Description:**
The Maestro Parent Portal keeps you connected to your child's music lessons without the back-and-forth texts with their teacher.

YOUR CHILD'S LESSON SCHEDULE
See every upcoming lesson at a glance — date, time, and what's coming up.

LESSON NOTES & ASSIGNMENTS
Read exactly what your child worked on in each lesson and what they should practice before the next one. Notes appear the moment their teacher saves them.

BALANCE & PAYMENTS
See your current prepaid balance and full payment history. Always know where you stand.

YOUR TEACHER SETS YOU UP
Ask your music teacher to add you to Maestro. They'll send you login credentials and you're in within minutes.

**Keywords:** music lessons, parent portal, lesson notes, music teacher, student progress, practice assignments

**Category:** Education

**Rating:** 4+
